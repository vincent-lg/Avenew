Welcome to Avenew One!

Avenew One is a MUD, a text-based game you can play online with multiple other players. It is based in a modern-day setting, a not-so-quiet fictional city in the southwestern parts of the United States.

{% if webclient_enabled %} You can start playing right away in your browser, with the online client. {% endif %} If you prefer playing with your favorite MU* client, you can instead connect to:

  • Host name: avenew.one
  • Telnet port: 4000 (no encryption)
  • Telnet-SSL port: 4003 (with encryption)

You will use port 4000 or 4003 depending on whether your MU* client supports telnet-SSL.
